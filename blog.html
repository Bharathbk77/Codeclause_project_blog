Ross Island-Andaman and Nicobar

Ross Island is a small yet significant island located in the Andaman and Nicobar archipelago in India. Once the administrative headquarters of the British Raj in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Ross Island is now a popular tourist destination known for its rich history and natural beauty.The island is known for its stunning beaches, clear waters, and lush green forests. Visitors can take a leisurely walk through the island's dense forests and spot a variety of birds and wildlife, including peacocks, deer, and exotic birds.

Darjeeling-West Bengal

Darjeeling is a picturesque town located in the Indian state of West Bengal. Known for its stunning views of the Himalayan mountain range, Darjeeling is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The town is famous for its tea plantations, and visitors can take a tour of the tea estates to learn about the tea-making process and sample some of the finest teas in the world.One of the major attractions in Darjeeling is the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, also known as the Toy Train. This narrow-gauge railway has been designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offers a scenic journey through the town's picturesque landscape. Darjeeling is also home to several monasteries, including the Ghoom Monastery and the Yiga Choeling Monastery. These monasteries are important centers of Tibetan Buddhism and offer a glimpse into the local culture and traditions.Darjeeling is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the beauty of the Himalayas and immerse themselves in the local culture and traditions of West Bengal

Yercaud is a Hill station in Salem District, Tamil Nadu

Yercaud is a beautiful hill station in Tamil Nadu, which is situated around a scenic mountain range. It is known for its unique flora, including rare orchids and silver oak trees, which add to the beauty of the region. Yercaud is a popular destination for trekking and adventure activities, making it a great spot for families with children. The area has several viewpoints, which offer stunning vistas of the surrounding landscape. The Yercaud Lake is also a major attraction, where visitors can enjoy boating and other water sports. The local culture is also worth experiencing, with traditional festivals and dances adding to the charm of the region. Overall, Yercaud is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Varanasi-Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Benares, is a city that is steeped in history and culture. Located in the state of Uttar Pradesh, it is one of the oldest and holiest cities in India, and is considered to be a center of spirituality and religion. The city is famous for its ghats, which are a series of steps leading down to the banks of the Ganges River. The ghats are a hub of activity, with pilgrims taking holy dips in the river and performing rituals. Varanasi is also home to numerous temples, including the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, which is dedicated to Lord Shiva. The city is known for its delicious street food and vibrant bazaars, where you can find everything from silk sarees to intricate handicrafts. A boat ride on the Ganges is a must-do activity in Varanasi, offering a unique perspective of the city and its ghats. The city's rich history and culture, combined with its spiritual significance, make it a must-visit destination for anyone interested in exploring ancient Indian traditions.

Munnar - Kerala

Munnar is a beautiful hill station located in the state of Kerala in India. Known for its stunning landscapes, tea plantations, and cool climate, Munnar is a popular tourist destination in India.

It is amazing place! The food was incredible and the scenery was breathtaking. I especially loved the pasta dishes and gelato. Here's a photo of me in front of the Colosseum.Munnar rises as three mountain streams merge - Mudrapuzha, Nallathanni and Kundala. 1,600 m above sea level, this hill station was once the summer resort of the erstwhile British Government in South India. One of the most sought after honeymoon destinations in Kerala, Munnar is replete with resorts and logding facilities that fit a wide rage of budgets. Sprawling tea plantations, picturesque towns, winding lanes and holiday facilities make this a popular resort town. Among the exotic flora found in the forests and grasslands here is the Neelakurinji. This flower which bathes the hills in blue once in every twelve years, will bloom next in 2030. Munnar also has the highest peak in South India, Anamudi, which towers over 2,695 m.
